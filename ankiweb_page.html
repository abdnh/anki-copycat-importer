This add-on allows you to import decks from copycat apps such as AnkiApp and AnkiPro.

AnkiApp

The add-on works by downloading your decks from the AnkiPro site. Go to Tools > Copycat Importer > Import from AnkiApp and follow the instructions.

Known Issues
  • Study progress is not imported.
AnkiPro

The add-on works by downloading your decks from the AnkiPro site. Go to Tools > Copycat Importer > Import from AnkiPro and follow the instructions.

Known Issues
  • Importing of library decks is not supported. Since they are simply scraped from AnkiWeb, you can search and download the decks from AnkiWeb instead.
  • Importing of study progress and deck options is not supported yet.
  • AnkiPro is trying to prevent people from exporting their decks using this add-on. You can use this Chrome extension to get your login token then paste it in the text field if you're having trouble logging in via the add-on.
